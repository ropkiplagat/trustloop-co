The Process

Step by Step

1

Applicant Downloads Their M-Pesa Statement

The borrower opens the official Safaricom M-Pesa app, navigates to "Statements", and downloads their PDF for the last 3–6 months. Free and instant. Works on any Android or iPhone.

💡 No M-Pesa app? They can also request a statement via *334# or from any Safaricom agent.
2

Statement Uploaded to TrustLoop Application Form

The applicant (or your loan officer on their behalf) uploads the PDF on TrustLoop's application form — takes under 60 seconds on a smartphone. The form also captures basic loan details: amount, purpose, tenure.

3

M-Pesa Intelligence Engine™ Analyses 47 Signals

Our scoring engine reads every transaction across the statement — income flows, spending patterns, M-Shwari and Fuliza behaviour, bill payments, and more. Grouped into 4 weighted dimensions.

Processing time: 60–90 seconds.

4

Score Delivered to Your Dashboard + SMS to Applicant

Your loans committee sees the full result on the TrustLoop dashboard: score (300–850), rating, recommendation, and a breakdown of the 5 most influential signals. The applicant receives an SMS notification simultaneously.

Privacy & Security

Your Borrower's Data Is Never Shared

The applicant uploads their own M-Pesa statement — we never connect directly to Safaricom or M-Pesa. The PDF is processed and the raw data is never stored unencrypted.

  • ✓ 256-bit TLS encryption in transit
  • ✓ PDF deleted after scoring — not stored long-term
  • ✓ No data sharing with third parties
  • ✓ GDPR-aligned data handling practices
  • ✓ Applicant consent captured on application form
