Why We Built This

The Problem We Couldn't Ignore

Kenya's SACCOs hold over KES 714 billion in loans — and lose a significant portion every year to preventable defaults. Most of those defaults come down to one thing: the loan officer had no way to see the borrower's real financial behaviour.

Payslips show what someone earns. M-Pesa shows how they live. Until TrustLoop, there was no tool that could read M-Pesa the way a trained credit analyst would — for any SACCO in Kenya, at any size, at an affordable price.

Abraham saw this gap from inside Kenya's cooperative sector. Rop built the engine to close it.
