## 5. BRAND VOICE GUIDE (Kenya SACCO Audience)

### Archetype: The Sage meets The Hero
- **Sage**: We know Kenya's credit market deeply — data, research, benchmarks
- **Hero**: We're fighting for the borrowers bureaus have abandoned

### Voice (constant across all touchpoints)
**Confident. Direct. Local. Respectful.**

---

### 5 DOs

| # | Rule | Example |
|---|------|---------|
| DO 1 | **Speak in KES, not USD** | "From KES 8,000/month" — not "$60/month." Kenya is the market. |
| DO 2 | **Name the problem precisely** | "Your NPL rate is eating your member dividends" — not "credit risk management challenges." |
| DO 3 | **Use M-Pesa as a shared reference** | "We read what M-Pesa already knows" — every Kenyan understands M-Pesa intimately. |
| DO 4 | **Show the number, not the concept** | "2 minutes. 47 signals. 1 decision." — SACCO managers are pragmatic. |
| DO 5 | **Acknowledge the human in every loan** | "Behind every application is a Kenyan trying to build something." — not cold SaaS language. |

---

### 5 DON'Ts

| # | Rule | Example |
|---|------|---------|
| DON'T 1 | **Never say "disrupt," "revolutionise," or "game-changing"** | Sounds like a pitch deck, not a tool. SACCO managers are sceptical of buzzwords. |
| DON'T 2 | **Never position AI as replacing the loan officer** | "AI-powered" is fine; "replaces your team" creates fear and kills deals. |
| DON'T 3 | **Never use Western fintech jargon** | Avoid "frictionless onboarding," "seamless UX," "best-in-class." Say "simple," "fast," "easy to set up." |
| DON'T 4 | **Never make claims you can't defend with data** | "Reduces defaults by up to 40%" — say "up to" or cite source. SACCOs will ask. |
| DON'T 5 | **Never ignore the WhatsApp channel** | SACCO managers prefer WhatsApp over email for follow-up. CTAs should lead to WhatsApp first, email second. |
