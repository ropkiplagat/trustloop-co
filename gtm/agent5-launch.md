### "Kenya SACCO Default Rate Benchmark Report 2026"

---

### What It Is
A 12–16 page research report giving SACCO managers and fintech lenders a clear benchmark for non-performing loan (NPL) rates across Kenya's cooperative and digital lending sector in 2026. Positions TrustLoop Africa as the authority on Kenya credit risk data.

---

### What It Contains

#### Section 1: Executive Summary (1 page)
- Kenya's average SACCO NPL rate in 2026 (benchmark: 6.8%)
- Comparison: Urban vs. peri-urban SACCOs
- Key finding: SACCOs using digital scoring tools average 3.1% NPL vs. 8.2% for manual-only

#### Section 2: The State of Kenyan SACCO Lending (2 pages)
- Total SACCO loan book size (KES 714B as of 2025)
- Loan growth rate vs. default rate trend (2021–2026)
- SASRA enforcement actions related to NPL thresholds
- % of SACCOs at risk of regulatory action based on NPL

#### Section 3: Default Rate Benchmarks by Segment (3 pages)
- By SACCO size (< 1,000 members / 1,000–5,000 / 5,000+)
- By county (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret)
- By loan type (salary advance / business / emergency / asset)
- By credit review method (manual / bureau / AI-assisted)

#### Section 4: Root Causes of Defaults in Kenya (2 pages)
- Top 5 reasons loans go bad (income shock, over-lending, identity fraud, guarantor failure, no early warning system)
- The "M-Pesa blind spot" — why bureau data misses 60% of real financial behaviour
- Case study: How a Nairobi-based SACCO reduced defaults by 43% in 2 quarters

#### Section 5: The 2026 Digital Credit Scoring Landscape (2 pages)
- What scoring tools are available in Kenya and what they cover
- Comparison table: CRB Bureau vs. M-Pesa scoring vs. internal Excel models
- Regulatory outlook: SASRA's digital lending guidelines for 2026–2027

#### Section 6: Action Plan for Your SACCO (2 pages)
- 5-step "Default Rate Reduction Checklist" (teasers TrustLoop's capabilities)
- How to calculate your SACCO's real NPL exposure
- What a 1% NPL reduction is worth in KES (calculator table by loan book size)

#### Appendix: Data Sources + Methodology (1 page)
- SASRA annual report, Kenya Cooperative Alliance data, KUSCCO benchmarks
- TrustLoop's internal dataset (anonymised, from pilot SACCOs)
