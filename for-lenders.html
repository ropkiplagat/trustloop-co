What We Analyse

The 47 M-Pesa Signals

Grouped into 4 dimensions — each weighted to reflect real Kenyan borrower behaviour.

Payment History (35%)Loan repayment timing, M-Shwari repayments, Fuliza clearance frequency
Income Flow (30%)Salary regularity, business income patterns, M-Pesa receive frequency
Credit Utilisation (20%)Fuliza usage ratio, overdraft frequency, net cash position trends
Loan-to-Income (15%)Requested amount vs. average monthly inflow, affordability ratio
Transaction Depth6-month history minimum, 12-month preferred
Bill PaymentsKPLC, Nairobi Water, school fees consistency
M-Shwari BehaviourSave & borrow history, lock savings patterns
Spend RegularityFood, transport, airtime patterns — stability indicators
